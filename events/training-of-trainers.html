Training of Trainer's Programme on Management of Animals in Emergencies at Guwahati, May 25,2015


Inaugural program of the 6 day "Training of Trainer's Programme on Management of Animals in Emergencies" jointly organized by the Policy Perspective Foundation (PPF), World Animal Protection (WAP) and NDRF was held at College of Veterinary Science, Guwahati on May 25,2015. This Inaugural function was presided over by Dr. B Barman, Minister for Agriculture, Assam with Shri. Kiren Rijiju, MOS (Home) as the Chief Guest and Shri. Engti, Minister for Animal Husbandry, Assam as the Guest of Honor. Others on the dais were OP Singh (DG NDRF), KM Singh (PPF), Gajendra Sharma (Country Director, WAP) and VC of Assam Agriculture University, Jorhat. This is the first of the 6 such programs to be organized tentatively by March 31, 2016.
